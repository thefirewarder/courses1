Fireward Courses (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the website firewardgames.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our site and digital products.

4. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to analyze usage and personalize your experience. Google AdSense may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to third-party pages such as Gumroad, YouTube, or other platforms. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

6. Children’s Privacy

Fireward Courses is designed for general audiences and educational use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your stored information by emailing fireward.helpdesk@gmail.com.

8. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Updates take effect immediately when posted on this page.
